This is not a book of answers. It is a book of questions. The AI can give you answers. Only you can provide the questions that matter.

Part I: The Detox

Before we can partner with the machine, we must clear the noise from our own signal.

Exercise 1: The Inertia Check

Identify three tasks you have delegated to AI (or others) simply because you didn't want to think about them. Be honest.

Ask yourself: Did delegating these make me freer, or did it make me numb?

Part II: The Mirror

Your interactions with AI are a mirror of your own clarity and intent. Let's look into the glass.

Exercise 2: The Tone Audit

Look at your last 5 prompts. Were they commands ("Do this") or requests ("Help me with this")?
